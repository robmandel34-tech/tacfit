Inside the App

A wellness platform that actually pulls you in.

Not just another workout tracker. TacFit is built around the people who keep you going.

See your team's every move.

The Intel Feed is a live stream of every workout, every win, every shared moment from your squad. Cheer each other on, like, comment, stay connected.

Pick a challenge. Commit.

Browse active competitions, see the rules, the goals, the join window. One tap and you and your team are in — chasing the same goal together.

Your squad. Your tribe.

Form your team, set your name, set your motto. Track progress as a unit. Win as a unit. This is the part that makes you actually show up.

Stay in the conversation.

Team comms keep the energy flowing between workouts. Drop a meme, plan a meetup, assign mission tasks. Real chat for real teams.

Race to victory.

Watch your team climb the mountain. The progress map turns weeks of workouts into a journey you can see — base camp to summit.
